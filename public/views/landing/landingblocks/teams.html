Our mouse-sized Micromouse robot scurries around a maze looking for the fastest path to the center. It uses a combination of motor control/feedback algorithms and distance sensors.

UAS Drone
A quadcopter that will fly autonomously, avoid obstacles, and complete aerial missions. For the UAS competition, set for next year, we must build a drone capable of swift aerial maneuvers in addition to image analysis software that can detect, analyze, and identify objects on the ground. Prepare for an intense robot dogfight.
